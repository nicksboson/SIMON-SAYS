How to Play Simon Says

Game Rules:

  1. Watch the sequence of colors that light up
  2. Repeat the sequence by clicking/touching the colors in the same order
  3. Each level adds one more color to the sequence
  4. If you make a mistake, the game ends
  5. Try to reach the highest level possible!

Controls:

  • Start: Click the "Start Game" button
  • Play: Click or touch the colored buttons
  • Restart: After game over, click "Try Again"
